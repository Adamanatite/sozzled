  1. Drink responsibly. Every player must be over the legal drinking age and should agree on a limit of drinks for a player on any one turn (No more than 10).

Some cards require blanks to be filled in by the current player or the rest of the group. Examples are provided to help, but you may fill it in with whatever you like.
